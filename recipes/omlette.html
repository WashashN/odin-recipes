Low Calorie Omelette

Description

The Best Omelette Recipe is low in calories, and can be made vegetarian or with ham. You can cut more calories by using 3 egg whites in place of the 2 eggs, but I find this minor "splurge" in ingredients makes a far superior tasting omelet recipe.

Ingredients:

  • olive oil spray
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cheese sharp cheddar, feta, or goat cheese
  • 4 cherry tomatoes quartered
  • 1 tablespoon diced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons diced ham

Cooking steps:

  1. Lightly spray a small skillet with olive oil spray and place over medium heat
  2. n a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs with 1 teaspoon of water and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the eggs into the hot skillet and cook, undisturbed, until they start to bubble up and pull away from the sides of the pan.
  4. Add 1/2 of the cheese on one side of the omelette, top with veggies and ham (if using) and remaining cheese.
  5. Fold the bare side over over the side with the fillings and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook for 2 minutes more before moving to a plate. Serve hot.

Nutritional values:

  • Kcal : 232
  • Fat : 15.3g
  • Carbs: 6.4g
  • Prot : 17.6g
